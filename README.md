**1.Airbnb Global Performance insights dashboard**

A dynamic, interactive data visualisation tool built to explore Airbnb performance data for over 250k listings in 10 major cities, including information about hosts, pricing, location, and room type, along with over 5 million historical reviews.

**2.Description**

The Airbnb Global Performance Insights Dashboard provides a centralized, data-driven view of how listings perform across different regions, focusing on both listing metrics and guest reviews.It enables users to monitor key indicators such as occupancy rates, pricing trends, and booking performance over time.The dashboard offers a comprehensive global view that combines operational performance with customer sentiment, supporting strategic decisions to optimize listings, enhance guest experience, and maximize revenue.

**3.Tech Stack**

The dashboard was built using the following tools and technologies:

_Power BI Desktop_- Main data visualisation platform used for report creation.

_Power Query_- Data transformation and cleaning layer for reshaping and preparing the data.

_DAX(Data Analysis Expressions_)- Used for calculated measures, dynamic visuals, and continual logic.

_Data Modeling_- Relationship established among tables(listings, reviews) to enable cross-filtering and aggregation.

_File Format_-.pbix for development and .png fro dashboard previews.

**4.Data Source**

https://mavenanalytics.io/data-playground/airbnb-listings-reviews 

**5.Business problem**

Airbnb hosts and platform stakeholders lack a centralized, data-driven way to understand and optimize listing performance at scale, especially across diverse global markets with varying demand patterns, competition levels, and guest expectations.

**6.Key Questions**

How are company's listings performing over time in terms of bookings, occupancy, and revenue?

Which listings are top-performing vs. underperforming, and why?

What is the conversion rate from views to bookings?

How does price impact occupancy and bookings?

How do performance metrics vary across different markets or seasons?

Which types of listings (entire home, private room, luxury, budget) perform best?

Do higher ratings lead to better conversion and occupancy?

What actions will maximize revenue while maintaining guest satisfaction?

**7.Goal of the dashboard**

The goal of the Airbnb Global Performance Insights Dashboard is to enable data-driven decision-making by providing a clear, comprehensive view of listing performance and guest experience across global markets. It help hosts and stakeholders optimize bookings, improve guest satisfaction, and maximize revenue by understanding what drives performance.

**8.Key visuals**

_a.New listings(line chart)_

Showing:Take-off point,Peak point,Precovid new listings

_b.Market share by city(line and stacked column chart)_

Showing:City contribution,Market share trends,Market concentration,Decline detection,Growth identification

_c.Room types vs average price(stacked bar chart)_

Showing:Price comparison across room types like entire room, hotel room, shared room, private room,Contribution of different cities to pricing,High value markets,Contribution of each room type to revenue potential

_d.City vs average rating (stacked column chart)_

Showing:Cities having highest and lowest guest satisfaction,Contribution of aspects like cleanliness and value for money to ratings

_e.City vs rating- categoiries(matrix table)_

Showing:Direct city comparison,Category level (accuracy, cleanliness, communication, location, value) performance,Consistency of experience(Cities with uniformly high scores → consistent quality,Large variation → inconsistent guest experience)

_f.Reviewers vs reviews per reviewer(line and stacked column chart)_

Showing:Reviewer vlome and repeat behaviour,Growth in user engagement,Engagement drop

_g.seasonaltiy(% of monthly reviews in each moth in each city) (ribbon chart)_

Showing:Seasonal travel trends,City ranting over time,Gain/loss of share by city month over month 

**9.Business impact and insights**

_a.Revenue Growth_:This dashboard enables company to use optimized pricing strategies based on demand trends and market comparisons

_b.Improved Occupancy Rates_:Higher booking rates and better utilization of listings by identifying factors causing low conversion or booking drop-offs

_c.Enhanced Guest Experience_:Converts reviews into actionable insights (e.g., cleanliness, communication issues) resulting into Higher ratings, better reviews, and increased repeat bookings

_d.Competitive Advantage_:Helps hosts stay competitive in pricing, quality, and service providing stronger market positioning

_e.Scalable Growth for Multi-Listing Hosts_:Allows property managers to monitor performance across multiple regions and listings resulting into efficient scaling and portfolio optimization
